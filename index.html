So who am I, really?

Musings about the Fermi Paradox and the possibilities of civilizations and life outside our solar system. Creating an album using GarageBand and releasing it on Spotify. Binge-watching late-night talk shows and comedian standups to get my daily dose of laughs while developing my public speaking skills. Shamelessly indulging in video gaming with Rocket League and Fortnite. Reflections on the impact that AI and ML will have on society in the future: virtual reality gaming, self-driving cars, autonomous robotics, and Big Data manipulation. Immersion in Bollywood culture through Raas and other dance forms.

As an older brother and the first of my family to go to college outside of Florida, I look to my inspirations -- family, friends, and celebrity influences -- to guide me in my life: to a life of exploration, curiosity, and happiness. I'm proud of my heritage, my hometown, my high school experiences, of where I am right now, and believe I will be proud of what I become.
